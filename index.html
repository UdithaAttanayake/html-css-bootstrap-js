USER RESEARCH

During the last 20 years, the government has tried ample of ways to tackle the waste management strategies for the country. That includes,

  • Burning garbage
  • Energy projects
  • Garbage dumping
  • Pitting garbage in layers(with soil)
  • Moving garbage
  • Sanitary landfilling

    • Unfortunately, the lack of knowledge,Rules, law regulation and environmental standards lead it to inconsistent and ineffective practices, a lack of accountable authorities and disinterested citizens.

    Sri Lanka is still unable to manage its own waste which is generated at local, provincial and national levels.

    We cannot continue it like this because,With increasing Industrialization, urbanization,population growth and increased consumption.

    Annual waste generation is expected to grow by 70% in 2050. So we need to perform a proper waste management system parallel with this ongoing development.

    The reason for this process is not being effective is it costs an immense amount of money to collect garbage and transport it to the garbage dumping areas. Rather than for waste management treatment.

    The main issue is going to be solid waste. Nowadays the government got the responsibility to separate the waste as perishable and nonperishables (Noncompostbales-plastic, polythene,glass, metal). The taxonomy process doing well than in the past. We need to process the projects after the taxonomy.

    Concerning the distribution and implementation of new technology that can create better benefits and impacts of the solution undertaken.

    Currently, projects are not working well for this large quantity of waste individually people should know new technologies & knowledge about new projects according to the 3R principle(Reuse, Recycle, Reduce).

    Encourage the participation of stakeholders and provide info about needed facts.

    Should have proper collection, transport & taxonomy programs for the waste, because this solid waste going to be elements of new products and the amount of waste going to be reduced.

    Research that is currently happening in society, this problem is worth solving.
